Controls: 
r to reset the position of the cow.
s to stop/start the spotlight panning
p to stop/start the point rotation around (0,0,0)

right click mouse and drag for rotation (x and y axis),
left/right arrow to rotate around z axis

left click the mouse and drag for translation (x and y axis)
up/down arrow to translate along z axis

Note: the cutoff angle is left at 7.50 degrees to match the required appearence of the cow
on the last part.
